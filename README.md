# Online Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Online Retail dataset from a UK-based retail company. The objective was to clean raw transactional data, perform exploratory data analysis (EDA), and generate actionable business insights.

---

## Objectives

- Clean and validate raw data
- Handle missing values and duplicates
- Standardize data formats
- Create new analytical features
- Perform exploratory data analysis
- Generate business recommendations

---

## Dataset

Source:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vijayuv/onlineretail

The dataset contains:

- Invoice Number
- Product Description
- Quantity
- Invoice Date
- Customer ID
- Unit Price
- Country

## Exploratory Data Analysis

The analysis answers the following business questions:

1. What are the top-selling products?

2. Which countries generate the highest revenue?

3. What is the monthly sales trend?

4. Who are the highest-value customers?

5. What is the distribution of unit prices?

---

## Business Insights

- The UK contributes the highest revenue.
- Revenue exhibits seasonal trends.
- A small percentage of customers generate a significant share of revenue.
- Several products dominate total sales volume.
- Pricing is concentrated around lower-value products.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve customer retention through loyalty programs.
- Expand marketing efforts into high-performing international markets.
- Optimize inventory for best-selling products.
- Forecast demand using seasonal trends.
- Focus promotional campaigns on high-value customer segments.
